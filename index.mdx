## What is Recepta.ai?

Recepta.ai is a comprehensive AI-powered communication platform that transforms how businesses handle customer interactions. Our platform automatically creates intelligent voice agents and chat widgets when you sign up, enabling 24/7 customer service with human-like conversations. Powered by advanced AI integrations including Voice AI, we provide seamless voice interactions, real-time chat capabilities, and comprehensive call management solutions.

**AI-Powered with Human Backup**: While our AI agents handle the majority of interactions intelligently, we provide seamless live transfer capabilities to human agents when complex situations require the human touch, ensuring your customers always receive the best possible service.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up & Automatic Setup">
    Create your account and complete the onboarding wizard. Your AI voice agent and chat widget are automatically created based on your business information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Agent Behavior">
    Customize how your agents handle different caller types (potential clients, existing customers, sales, others) with specific qualification questions and routing rules.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Business Knowledge">
    Upload your FAQs, business information, and directions. Add knowledge bases from documents, websites, or text content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Your Phone Number">
    Provision a new phone number or import existing ones. Assign agents to handle inbound and outbound calls.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy Chat Widget">
    Copy the generated embed code and add it to your website for instant chat support.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor & Optimize">
    Track call analytics, review quality scores, analyze transcripts, and continuously improve agent performance.
  </Step>
</Steps>
